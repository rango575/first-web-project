About us

Mullar Cycle Mart is a leading bicycle retailer that has been serving cycling enthusiasts for over five years. We are passionate about providing high-quality bicycles, accessories, and exceptional customer service to our valued customers. we understand the joy and freedom that comes with cycling. Whether you're a seasoned cyclist or just starting out on your cycling journey, we have a wide range of bicycles to suit every rider's needs and preferences. From road bikes and mountain bikes to hybrid bikes and electric bikes. Thank you for choosing Mullar Cycle Mart as your trusted bicycle retailer. We look forward to helping you find the perfect bike and providing you with exceptional service every step of the way. Happy cycling!
